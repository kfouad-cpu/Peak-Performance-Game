Peak Performance: 4-Week CycleAn interactive, strategy-based sports management game built with HTML5, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript.🏃 About the GameIn Peak Performance, you take on the role of an elite athlete in the final 4-week "Macrocycle" leading up to a major championship. You must balance three critical metrics:Physical Fatigue: Pushing too hard leads to injury (DNF).Mental Stress: High anxiety or burnout reduces training quality.Peak Performance: Your actual readiness for race day.🎮 How to PlayClone or Download: Simply download the index.html file.Open in Browser: Double-click index.html to play instantly—no installation required.Make Decisions: Each week (Foundation, Volume, Intensity, Taper), choose one of three training paths.Manage Scaling: As you get closer to the race, the stakes and fatigue costs increase significantly.🛠️ Technical DetailsFramework: Tailwind CSS (via CDN)Icons: Inline SVGLogic: Custom JavaScript state-management engineResponsive: Fully optimized for mobile and desktop play🏆 Winning ConditionsLegendary Gold: Performance > 95% with low fatigue.Olympic Silver/Bronze: High performance with moderate fatigue.The Crash: High performance but critical fatigue levels.DNF: Reaching 100% Physical or Mental fatigue.Created as an interactive training simulation.
